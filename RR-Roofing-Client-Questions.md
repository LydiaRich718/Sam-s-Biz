# R&R Roofing — Website Intake Questions

## Branding
1. Do you have a logo? If so, can you send the file?
2. Do you have preferred brand colors, or should we pick something that fits the storm/restoration vibe?

## Photos
3. Do you have your own photos of completed roofs or job sites? Or should we use stock photos?

## Licensing & Certifications
4. Are you currently licensed in PA and NJ, or is that still in progress?
5. Do you have a certification with Owens Corning, GAF, or CertainTeed? If so, can you send the certification number or badge?
6. Are you a registered roofing contractor with any state licensing board? Can you provide your license number(s)?

## Insurance & Restoration Process
7. Do you work directly with all insurance carriers, or only specific ones?
8. Do you assist homeowners with filing their claim, or do you just document the damage and let them handle it?
9. Do you do supplement work (going back to insurance for additional funds if more damage is found)?
10. Do you offer any warranty on your work? If so, what does it cover and for how long?

## Scheduling & Contact
11. For free estimate and inspection requests, do you want a simple contact form or a real booking calendar?
12. Should estimate and inspection requests go to the same email/phone, or be handled separately?

## Social & Reviews
13. Do you have a Google Business profile or Facebook page to link to?
14. Do you have 2-3 customer reviews or testimonials we can feature on the site?
